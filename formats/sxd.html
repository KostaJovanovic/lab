What is a .SXD file?

SXD is a StarOffice/OpenOffice Draw vector graphic.

Did you know
  • SXD was the drawing format of the OpenOffice 1 era.
  • SXD was used by both OpenOffice.org Draw and StarOffice, which shared the same OpenOffice.org XML format.
  • Its successor under OpenDocument is the ODG drawing format.
